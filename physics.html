Physics

I absolutely love physics, especially astrophysics and cosmology. I was turned on to physics by my 11th grade physics teacher, then I went out and bought In Search Of The Multiverse, by John Gribbin, and I was hooked. Although I don't want a career in academia, I think that my love of physics will somehow play into the rest of my life.

I think what draws me to physics is the incompleteness of it. No one knows why everything happens, and theories are being created and tested every day. I usually stay up to date reading select articles in Scientific American.

Fun Physics Facts

  1. The Moon recedes 1/2" from the Earth every 20 years!
  2. The Universe is expanding at an accelerating pace!
  3. There are about 300 sextillion stars in the Universe!
  4. Time and space are meaningless at the surface of a black hole!
  5. The Universe is about 13.7 billion years old!
I have also tried to be an engineer, and I competed in a science fair in my Junior year, but I think that I enjoy the pure learning and theoretical sides of physics.
